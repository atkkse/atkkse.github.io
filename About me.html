CV Sören 2018
(swedish)

My name is Sören Attermo.
I am working with testing / commissioning of electrical powerstations.
I have more than 20 years experience of electrical power and I have worked with: distribution, substations, medium voltage of electrical power, CHP:s, turbines, generators and low voltage switchgear.
When I am working I specialize especially with:
relay test, circuit / function testing of switch gear or transformers, but I am also giving technical advice to project leaders.
I have my own test equipment, but I can also rent special equipment if needed. I am self-employed, but in case of bigger projects I have the possibility to get help from colleagues in the same branch.
